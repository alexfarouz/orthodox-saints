Privacy Policy for Orthodox Saints

Last Updated: November 13, 2024

Summary: Orthodox Saints is a simple prayer app that works entirely offline. We don't collect, store, or share any of your data.

Overview

Orthodox Saints is a free iOS application that provides hourly prayer notifications and spiritual guidance from Orthodox Christian saints.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information.

The app operates entirely on your device and does not:

  • Collect personal data
  • Track user behavior
  • Share information with third parties
  • Require user accounts or registration
  • Access your contacts, location, or other device data

Notifications

The app schedules local notifications on your device to deliver hourly prayers. These notifications:

  • Are generated locally on your device
  • Do not send data to external servers
  • Can be disabled at any time in your device Settings

Widgets

The app provides home screen widgets that:

  • Display content locally from the app
  • Do not transmit any data
  • Update automatically based on the current hour

Data Storage

All app data, including saint images, prayers, notification schedules, and user preferences, is stored locally on your device only. No information is transmitted to external servers.

Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect any information from children. The app is suitable for all ages.

Third-Party Services

This app does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
